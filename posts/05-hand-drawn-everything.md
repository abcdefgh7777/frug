# hand drawn everything

no ai. no generators. no 10k collection from mixing 50 traits.

every pog is drawn by hand. my hand. one at a time.

took me weeks. could i have used midjourney? yeah. could i have coded a generator? probably. would it have soul? absolutely not.

theres something about hand drawn art that hits different. you can feel the imperfections. the wobbly lines. the slightly off colors.

thats not a bug. thats the feature.

each pog has personality because i gave it personality. not an algorithm.

when u use a pog pfp ur using something a real person sat down and created. not something a computer spit out in 3 seconds.

that matters to me. hope it matters to u too.
