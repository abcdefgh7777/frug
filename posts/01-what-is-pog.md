# what is pog?

ok so basically pog is just me drawing frogs

thats it. thats the whole thing.

i sat down one night, mass energy drink in hand, and started sketching these weird little frog dudes. didnt plan anything. didnt have a roadmap. didnt think about "utility" or whatever. just vibed and drew frogs.

100 of them actually. all hand drawn. every single one is different. some look angry. some look high. some look like they know something you dont.

theyre all free. no mint. no gas. no bullshit.

you like one? save it. use it. make it ur pfp. i literally do not care. thats the whole point.

pog is just art that wants to be used.
