# why i made this

been lurking ct for like 2 years now. seen so many pfp projects come and go. 99% of them are just cash grabs with zero soul.

mint for 0.1 eth. promise of metaverse land. discord with 50 roles. roadmap that goes nowhere.

i got tired of it.

so i thought... what if i just made something and gave it away? no strings attached. no floor price to worry about. no discord mods begging you to "engage for whitelist."

just art.

i draw. you use. we vibe.

thats it. thats why pog exists.

sometimes the simplest things hit the hardest.
