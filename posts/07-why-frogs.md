# why frogs tho

people keep asking why frogs.

honestly? i dont have a deep answer.

frogs are just funny looking. they sit weird. they have no business looking that smug. their whole vibe is just "i am here and i do not care."

plus crypto loves frogs. pepe. $pepe. frogger. countless others. theres something about frogs and internet culture that just works.

but mostly i just like drawing them. they have expressive faces. you can make a frog look happy, sad, angry, confused, enlightened, or absolutely zooted with just a few lines.

try doing that with a lion or whatever. way harder.

frogs are perfect meme canvases. thats the real answer.

pog is just me adding to the grand tradition of internet frogs.
